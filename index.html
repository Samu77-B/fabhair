Who is Fab Hair London?

Fab Hair London is one of North London's highest rated independent hair salons, established in 1997.
‍
Our goal is to combine high-end hairdressing with an exceptional standard of customer care.

  • Established in 1997 - over 27 years of experience
  • Over 500 five-star Google reviews
  • Specialising in creative colour, balayage, and precision cutting
  • Working exclusively with cruelty-free brands since 1997

We are excited to offer our customers a range of nourishing Paul Mitchell tea tree hair care solutions.

Our Paul Mitchell Tea Tree Scalp Care and Tea Tree Scalp Wellness products, featuring PCR packaging made from 100% post-consumer recycled materials, are now available in our shop.

With 100% Australian tea tree oil as the key ingredient, these effective hair care solutions can help promote a healthy scalp and beautiful hair.

Davines Group was founded in Parma, Italy in 1983 and ever since has surged in popularity, gaining a cult-like following. Davines formulations favour the use of ingredients of natural origin, are generated with renewable energy and are packaged to minimise environmental impact.

At Fab we now stock a range of Davines products, including best sellers like the Oi All In One Milk, as well as products for those who are environmentally conscious, such as shampoo bars and refillables.

For more information, please feel free to call us on 02088867070 or email us at salon@fabhair.london.

Services

What services does Fab Hair offer? Fab Hair specialises in all fields of hairdressing with an extensive range of services for every client.

Our services include:

  • Hair Cutting: Precision cuts from stylist to salon director level
  • Hair Colouring: Full head colour, root refresh, vivid colours, and colour correction
  • Balayage: Hand-painted highlights for natural-looking results
  • Highlights: Partial, half head, and full head foil highlights
  • Hair Extensions: Instant volume and length
  • Styling: Professional blow drys and styling services
  • Keratin Smoothing: Frizz-free, silky smooth hair treatments
  • Barbering: Men's haircuts and styling

We do it all.

Skin Testing

New clients require a skin test 48 hours before their first colour appointment, and returning clients require a skin test every 3 months without colour in the salon.

Deposits

We may request a deposit for certain appointments, deposits are fully refundable/transferable ONLY if the appointment is cancelled/rescheduled at least 72 hours prior to your appointment date.

Loyalty Cards

Earn 1 stamp per visit, plus additional stamps on high value services. Total cost of visit must total more than £20 to receive a stamp. Cannot be used in conjunction with other promotions. Children's services are excluded from this promotion.
